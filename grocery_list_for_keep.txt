Workout Meal Plan — Weekly Groceries
=====================================

☐ === PROTEINS ===
☐ Chicken breast (2-3 lbs) — boneless skinless, buy in bulk
☐ Salmon fillets (1.5-2 lbs) — wild-caught preferred
☐ Shrimp (1 lb, raw, peeled)
☐ Canned tuna in olive oil (4-6 cans)
☐ Lean ground turkey (1 lb)
☐ Eggs (2 dozen)
☐ Greek yogurt, plain full-fat (32 oz tub)
☐ Whey or casein protein powder (<5g sugar/serving)
☐ String cheese or part-skim mozzarella

☐ === PRODUCE ===
☐ Avocados (7-10) — one per day
☐ Baby spinach (large bag)
☐ Arugula (bag)
☐ Cherry tomatoes (2 pints)
☐ English cucumber (2)
☐ Broccoli (2 heads or bag of florets)
☐ Asparagus (2 bundles)
☐ Zucchini (3-4)
☐ Eggplant (1-2)
☐ Red onion (2)
☐ Garlic (1 head)
☐ Lemons (4-6)
☐ Blueberries or strawberries (1-2 pints)
☐ Cauliflower (1 head)

☐ === PANTRY / FATS / OILS ===
☐ Extra virgin olive oil (large bottle)
☐ Kalamata olives (jar)
☐ Raw almonds or walnuts (bag)
☐ Red wine vinegar
☐ Dijon mustard
☐ Canned chickpeas (3-4 cans)
☐ Canned white beans (2-3 cans)
☐ Quinoa (small bag — training days only)
☐ Roasted red peppers (jar)

☐ === HERBS & SPICES ===
☐ Sea salt + black pepper
☐ Dried oregano, thyme, rosemary
☐ Smoked paprika
☐ Cumin
☐ Fresh parsley or basil (bunch)

☐ === HYDRATION / ELECTROLYTES ===
☐ Electrolyte packets, no sugar (LMNT or Liquid IV Zero)
☐ Sparkling water (case)
☐ Black coffee or green tea
