⚠ Lower Back Rules — Read First
  • No barbell back squats or conventional deadlifts
  • All dumbbell work seated or supported — never standing under load with free spine
  • 5-min core activation warm-up before every session: dead bug + bird-dog + glute bridge
  • Brace core on every rep — treat your trunk like a lifting belt
  • STOP IMMEDIATELY if lower back recruits. No pushing through it.

Wednesday Swim
▼

30–45 minutes of lap swimming.

Mix freestyle and backstroke. Backstroke decompresses the lumbar spine and opens the thoracic region. On high back-sensitivity days, use a pull buoy for the entire session to keep all rotation gentle.

This session is cardio, active recovery, and back therapy simultaneously. Don't skip it.

12:00 PM – 8:00 PM · 16:8 Intermittent Fasting

Growth hormone peaks during the fast, insulin stays low. Breaking fast at noon keeps the morning productive and simplifies meal timing around training.

Meal 1 — 12:00 PM · Break Fast
Egg & Avocado Scramble
▼
Ingredients
  • 3 large eggs + 2 egg whites
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, red pepper flakes, squeeze of lemon
Steps
  1. Heat olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  2. Whisk eggs and egg whites together; season with salt and pepper.
  3. Pour into pan and stir gently with a spatula until just set, 2–3 min.
  4. Plate eggs alongside sliced avocado and cherry tomatoes.
  5. Finish with red pepper flakes and a squeeze of lemon.
Meal 2 — 3:00 PM
Grilled Chicken Arugula Salad
▼
Ingredients
  • 6 oz chicken breast
  • 2 cups arugula
  • ¼ cup Kalamata olives
  • ½ cup roasted red peppers, sliced
  • ¼ red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp red wine vinegar, 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt, pepper, dried oregano
Steps
  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and dried oregano.
  2. Grill or pan-sear over medium-high heat, 5–6 min per side until cooked through.
  3. Let rest 5 min, then slice.
  4. Toss arugula, olives, peppers, and red onion in a bowl.
  5. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, and Dijon; drizzle over salad.
  6. Top with sliced chicken and serve.
Meal 3 — 6:30 PM · Largest Meal
Herb-Crusted Salmon + Broccoli + White Beans
▼
Ingredients
  • 8 oz salmon fillet
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • ½ cup canned white beans, drained
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh dill, parsley, lemon zest, salt, pepper
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a sheet pan with foil.
  2. Mix olive oil, garlic, dill, parsley, and lemon zest; rub over salmon.
  3. Toss broccoli in olive oil, salt, and pepper; spread on pan alongside salmon.
  4. Roast 12–15 min until salmon is flaky and broccoli is tender.
  5. Warm white beans in a small pan with olive oil and a pinch of salt.
  6. Plate salmon over white beans with broccoli on the side.

Meal 2 — 3:00 PM
Shrimp & Spinach + Chickpeas
▼
Ingredients
  • 6 oz raw shrimp, peeled
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • ½ cup canned chickpeas, drained
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Lemon juice, red pepper flakes, salt
Steps
  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and red pepper flakes; sauté 1 min.
  3. Add shrimp; cook 2 min per side until pink. Remove and set aside.
  4. Add spinach and chickpeas; toss until spinach wilts, about 2 min.
  5. Return shrimp to pan; squeeze lemon juice over everything and toss.
Meal 3 — 6:30 PM · Largest Meal
Ground Turkey & Zucchini Skillet
▼
Ingredients
  • 8 oz lean ground turkey
  • 2 medium zucchini, diced
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Dried oregano, cumin, salt, pepper
Steps
  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add onion and garlic; sauté 2–3 min until softened.
  3. Add turkey; brown 5–6 min, breaking it up, until cooked through.
  4. Stir in zucchini, tomatoes, oregano, and cumin.
  5. Cook 4–5 min more until zucchini is tender. Season and serve.

Baked Chicken Thighs + Asparagus + Lentils
▼
Ingredients
  • 2 bone-in skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
  • ½ cup canned green lentils, drained
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Garlic powder, dried rosemary, salt, pepper
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.

Meal 1 — 12:00 PM · Break Fast
Shakshuka + Avocado
▼
Ingredients
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • ½ red onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp paprika, pinch of cayenne
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, pepper, fresh parsley
Steps
  1. Heat olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium heat.
  2. Sauté onion 3 min; add garlic, cumin, paprika, and cayenne; cook 1 min.
  3. Pour in crushed tomatoes; season with salt and pepper; simmer 5–7 min.
  4. Make 3 wells in sauce; crack an egg into each well.
  5. Cover and cook 5–7 min until whites are set and yolks are runny.
  6. Garnish with parsley; serve with sliced avocado on the side.

Meal 3 — 6:30 PM · Largest Meal
Shrimp Stir-fry + Broccoli + Quinoa
▼
Ingredients
  • 8 oz raw shrimp, peeled
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • ½ cup dry quinoa (cook per package → ~1 cup cooked)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce, few drops sesame oil, red pepper flakes
Steps
  1. Cook quinoa per package directions; set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a wok or large pan over high heat.
  3. Add garlic and broccoli; stir-fry 3–4 min until bright green.
  4. Add shrimp; cook 2 min per side until pink.
  5. Drizzle soy sauce and sesame oil; toss everything together.
  6. Serve over quinoa with red pepper flakes.

Slow-Roasted Salmon + Asparagus + Lentils
▼
Ingredients
  • 8 oz salmon fillet
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
  • ½ cup canned green lentils, drained
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon (juice + zest), fresh dill, garlic, salt, pepper
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 275°F.
  2. Rub salmon with 1 tbsp olive oil, lemon zest, dill, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roast salmon 25–30 min until just translucent in the center.
  4. Meanwhile, toss asparagus in olive oil; roast at 425°F for 12 min in a separate pan.
  5. Warm lentils with garlic and olive oil in a small saucepan.
  6. Plate salmon over lentils with asparagus. Squeeze lemon over all.

Herb-Roasted Chicken + Cauliflower + Chickpeas
▼
Ingredients
  • 2 bone-in chicken thighs or 1 large breast
  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • ½ cup canned chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Dried rosemary, thyme, paprika, salt, pepper
Steps
